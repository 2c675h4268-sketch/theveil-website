The Sightings Map

More than 80,000 reported UFO and UAP sightings, mapped across eight decades. Switch between a density heatmap and a by-country view, and scrub through time to watch the pattern shift.

Important: these are reported sightings, drawn mostly from the U.S.-based National UFO Reporting Center. Concentration reflects where reports are filed, driven by population, internet access, and English-language reporting, not where UAP actually are. The United States dominates the data because that is where the reporting infrastructure lives, which is a fact about reporting, not about the phenomena.
